About Us

The FIRST INSIGHT PROPERTY CO LTD is the premier gathering of top-level business leaders and decision-makers to exchange ideas, build partnerships, gain industry insights, and discover the best industry practices in ASEAN REGION.

Born with high vision to create as a high-level conference that aims to ‘educate, innovate and inspire’, the INTERNATIONAL PROPERTY EXPO is now part of FIRST INSIGHT PROPERTY, Asia’s foremost international Property Platform Network, covering the biggest issues driving real estate development, investment and growth in Asia and across the world, with particular focus on property technology and digital innovation.

We have invited Singapore, Cambodia, Vietnam, Malaysia, Philipine, Thailand and China Property Developer to join us on 27-29 March 2020 to exhibit and give talks and speeches in Marina Bay Sands.

The Property Expo is held annually, travelling each year to a new destination Singapore , Hong Kong, Thailand, Malaysia alongside the our magazine publication , gala dinner, and attracting leading speakers, industry influencers and experts from all the sectors that shape Asian real estate to refresh thinking, reset agendas and connect with peers.
